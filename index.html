Monitor the Rocky Shores

The health of the marine environment is reflected in its biodiversity. By continuously and frequently monitoring life on the rocky shores, we can follow short and long-term changes - equipping us with the information needed for fast-reaction interventions (such as after an oil spill/ noodle spill), and improve the management of the coastline
